Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Doles Enterprise ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use NextWave Automation ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to operate the Service:

  • OAuth access tokens — short-lived tokens issued by connected platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook) that allow the Service to publish content on your behalf. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Content metadata — titles, captions, hashtags, and scheduled publish times for videos you queue through the Service.
  • Publish logs — records of publishing activity including timestamps and platform responses, stored locally.

We do not collect personal identifiers beyond what is necessary to authenticate with connected platforms.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Authenticate with your authorized social media accounts.
  • Schedule and publish video content to those accounts on your behalf.
  • Log publishing outcomes for your review.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. TikTok Data

When you authorize the Service to access your TikTok account, we receive and store OAuth tokens issued by TikTok. We use these tokens only to publish video content through the TikTok Content Posting API. We do not access, store, or process any TikTok user profile data, follower information, analytics, or private messages. Tokens are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external server controlled by us.

4. Data Storage

All credentials and content metadata are stored locally on the device running the Service. We do not operate a centralized database of user data. Video files may be temporarily hosted on Cloudflare R2 object storage solely for the purpose of delivering them to connected platforms.

5. Third-Party Platforms

The Service interacts with third-party platforms including TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies and terms of service.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained until you disconnect the platform or delete the local token store. Publish logs are retained locally and may be deleted by the user at any time.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke the Service's access to any connected platform at any time through that platform's account settings. You may delete all locally stored data by removing the Service's data directory.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at dolesenterprise@gmail.com.
